Licensing

How Licensing Works

RightSet administers YouTube licensing programs on behalf of rights holders. Approved creators are granted permission to use the content into their videos, with royalties tracked, calculated, and distributed through our platform.

How it works

Three steps from enrollment to royalty payout.

STEP 01

Rights holders enroll their content.

Eligible rights holders enroll a library of content into a licensing program administered by RightSet.

STEP 02

Creators are vetted and approved.

Creators apply or are invited. Once vetted against the program's Creator Criteria, approved creators accept the Creator Licensing Terms through our platform.

STEP 03

Royalties tracked and paid.

Our platform tracks every use, calculates royalties owed under the program, and distributes the rights holder's share.

For creators

Use licensed content. Pay royalties. Stay claim-free.

Approved creators get permission to use licensed content in their videos in exchange for royalties. To qualify, you'll need to be YouTube-monetized and meet the program's requirements. Follow the guidelines, and you're exempt from Content ID claims on that content.
