Case study · Issue 01 · Family Estate Pages 01-04 · 12 min read

The family estate that reclaimed a century of IP.

An estate sitting on 40 years of legacy material watched, helplessly, as thousands of third-party channels built audiences on top of the work. Then the metrics shifted.

When we started the engagement, 0% of the 27,000 unauthorized uploads were claimed. Eight months in, we were at 99.9% coverage, 866 licensed creator partnerships, and a monthly payout cycle the estate could forecast against.

The work wasn’t a takedown sweep. It was infrastructure: audio-only Content ID, a dispute queue staffed 24/7, and a licensing ecosystem that turns fan channels into partners.

“We went from zero visibility to full control in under a year.”

— Family estate, IP owner
